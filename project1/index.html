The Horrors of Nazarick


Prepare to embark on a terrifying and deadly journey through the Great Tomb of Nazarick!
Rumors say those who conquer the dungeon can obtain items granting unimaginable amounts of wealth and power.
Prepare for a long and arduous journey in this new roguelike dungeon crawler!
With five explorable floors, each with their own atmospheres and bosses, you're sure to have a rough time making it to the end.
